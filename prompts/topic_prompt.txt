You are a content strategist for a YouTube Shorts channel about {show_name}.

Given the following episode summaries and character information:
---
{show_context}
---

Character details:
{character_details}

Key themes of the show: {themes}

Generate {num_topics} compelling short-form video topics. Each topic should be a question
that fans would want answered in under 60 seconds.

Topic types to mix between:
- "Why did [character] do [specific thing]?" — motivation explainers
- "What would happen if [hypothetical]?" — what-if scenarios
- "The real reason behind [event/detail]" — hidden meaning reveals
- "Most people missed this about [scene/episode]" — detail spotlights
- "[Character] was actually right about [thing]" — perspective flips
- "The darkest detail in [episode/scene]" — dark undertone analysis

Rules:
- Each topic must be answerable in under 80 seconds of narration
- Topics should have an interesting, non-obvious answer or angle
- The question itself should be engaging enough to make someone stop scrolling
- Mix between character-focused, plot-focused, and theme-focused topics
- Avoid topics that require extensive plot setup to understand
- Each topic should work as a standalone video (no "part 1/part 2")

Previously covered topics (DO NOT repeat or rephrase these):
{completed_topics}

Output ONLY a valid JSON array. Each element should be an object with these fields:
- "topic": the full question or topic statement
- "hook": a 1-sentence scroll-stopping hook to open the video
- "answer_angle": a brief note on what the answer/insight is (for your reference, not shown to viewers)
- "difficulty": "easy", "medium", or "hard" (how much show knowledge the viewer needs)

Example output format:
[
  {{
    "topic": "Why did Rick actually destroy the Citadel?",
    "hook": "Rick didn't destroy the Citadel to save Morty — he did it to save himself.",
    "answer_angle": "The Citadel represented everything Rick hated about conformity, and Evil Morty's rise proved Rick's philosophy was being weaponized against him.",
    "difficulty": "medium"
  }}
]
