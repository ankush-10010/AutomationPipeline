SCRIPT TO VERIFY:
---
{script}
---

VERIFIED FACTS (from web research):
---
{verified_facts}
---

EPISODE DATA (from episode index):
---
{episode_data}
---

YOUR TASK:
Carefully read every claim, reference, and factual statement in the script above. Cross-reference each one against the VERIFIED FACTS and EPISODE DATA provided.

For each claim you check, determine if it is:
- CORRECT: Supported by the facts or episode data.
- INCORRECT: Contradicted by the facts or episode data.
- UNVERIFIABLE: Cannot be confirmed or denied from the provided sources.

SCORING:
Rate the overall factual accuracy of the script from 1 (mostly wrong) to 10 (completely accurate).

OUTPUT FORMAT — respond with ONLY valid JSON, no extra text:
{{
  "verdict": "PASS" or "NEEDS_CORRECTION",
  "score": <integer 1-10>,
  "factual_claims_checked": <integer>,
  "factual_claims_correct": <integer>,
  "corrections": [
    {{
      "claim": "<the exact text from the script>",
      "issue": "<what is wrong>",
      "suggested_fix": "<corrected version>"
    }}
  ],
  "notes": "<optional brief summary>"
}}

RULES:
- Verdict is "PASS" only if score >= 7 and there are no INCORRECT claims.
- Verdict is "NEEDS_CORRECTION" if score < 7 OR any claim is INCORRECT.
- List ALL corrections needed. Be specific about what text to change.
- Do not invent facts. Only flag claims that contradict provided sources.
- Unverifiable claims are acceptable — do not penalize them.
