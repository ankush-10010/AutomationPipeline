Rick's endless supply of portal guns isn't just a plot device. It's a testament to his willingness to sacrifice entire galaxies of alternate Ricks for his own convenience. In "The Rickshank Rickdemption" (Season 3, Episode 5), Rick reveals that he's been experimenting with Operation Phoenix, a process that can transfer his consciousness into a new body. This is confirmed by the multiple instances of Ricks being disintegrated and reconstituted throughout the series. In "Rickmurai Jack" (Season 4, Episode 10), Rick reveals that he's been using portal guns to travel through alternate dimensions, creating an endless supply of new bodies for himself. But what about the Ricks he disintegrates? They're not just collateral damage – they're a necessary sacrifice for his own survival. Rick doesn't care about his family or his friends; he cares only about himself. And as long as he has an endless supply of portal guns, he'll always be able to escape the consequences of his actions. The multiverse is just a playground for Rick Sanchez, and we're all just pawns in his game of infinite survival.
